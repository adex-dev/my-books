title: Introduction to Google Sheets
description: Google Sheets is a cloud-based spreadsheet application by Google used for processing data online. It allows users to create tables, perform automatic calculations, manage data, and create collaborative reports within a single document in real-time.
---

With support for various formulas such as `IF`, `ARRAYFORMULA`, `VLOOKUP`, `QUERY`, and many others, Google Sheets is commonly used for:

* Data processing
* Financial reports
* Monitoring dashboards
* Inventory management
* Administrative task automation
* Integration with other systems through APIs or Apps Script

The main advantages of Google Sheets are its ease of collaboration, accessibility from anywhere, and direct integration with other Google services.
